Delete Your Aerocred Account and Data

At Aerocred, we respect your privacy and your right to delete your data at any time.

How to Request Account Deletion

  1. Send an email to info@aerocred.com with the subject "Account Deletion Request".
  2. Include your registered email address or username in the message.
  3. We will verify your identity and process your request within 7 business days.

What Data Will Be Deleted?

  • Account information (name, email, phone number)
  • Usage history
  • Stored preferences and settings

What Data Might Be Retained?

In some cases, we may retain data for legal or regulatory reasons (e.g., financial transactions or audit logs). This data will be kept for the minimum required period and protected securely.
